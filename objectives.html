Learning Objectives

Develop Practical Skills

Provides hands-on experience, helping students develop practical skills in event coordination, communication, and problem-solving, which are valuable in both academic and professional settings.

Enhance Technical Skills

Engaging in lifelong learning, particularly in the evolving field of electrical engineering, enhances technical knowledge. By participating in seminars, students stay informed about advancements and apply new concepts, contributing to their expertise and professional competence.

Professional Growth

Develops positive attitudes, responsibility, and behavior in students helps professional growth. By managing tasks like organizing seminars, students build leadership, time management, and accountability skills essential for career success in electrical engineering.
